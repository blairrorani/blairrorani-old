Hi, I'm Blair Rorani

I help companies visualise and explain software, processes and ideas that so they're easier to understand.

The default way to explain things in business is to use crowded PowerPoint slides, boring technical overviews, jargon-filled emails and a lot of words.

What if you had a simpler, more enaging way to explain new software, business processes and big ideas?

I use visual artefacts, stories, games and other engaging, fun stuff to make information about your business easier to understand and apply.

How I help

I teach workshops, coach teams and create custom visual explanations. Here's what I offer.

Think

I clarify what your team is building with maps, sketches, storyboards and visual prototypes.

Communicate

I tell the story of your new software, process or idea with slides, illustrations and animations.

Teach

I design practical, visual guides and challenges to get your your team and your customers up to speed.
